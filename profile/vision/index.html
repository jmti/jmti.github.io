Management Principles From Fukuoka to Asia to the World.
Contributing to the development of a creative and hopeful information society by providing state-of-art technology solutions.


Business Policy Our goal is to be a technology solution company that develops new values in partnership with customers, business partners, our employees and local communities, remaining responsive to our ever-changing information society.
Today's information society is dynamic and changes continuously.


Based on our company's policy of adhering to joint creation in the development of new values, we will commit to the development/growth of customers, business partners, our employees and local communities through the provision of technology solution services.
 
1) Joint creation with customers
We will develop structures and systems to be followed in the process of joint value creation while providing high-quality technology solution services.

2) Joint creation with business partners
We will strengthen the partnership with our business partners following the concept that partners are essential structural components in joint value creation.

3) Joint creation with our employees
Based on our belief that the development of human resources is part of our company's mission, we have a goal to invest in our employees while, at the same time, help the company grow.

  4) Joint creation with local communities
  Focusing on a goal of enhancing our company's corporate reputation, we will contribute to the development of local communities.
