Our goal is to be a technology solution company that develops new values in partnership with customers, business partners, our employees and local communities, remaining responsive to our ever-changing information society.
Today's information society is dynamic and changes continuously.

　

Based on our company's policy of adhering to joint creation in the development of new values, we will commit to the development/growth of customers, business partners, our employees and local communities through the provision of technology solution services.

　  
　 1) Joint creation with customers
　

We will develop structures and systems to be followed in the process of joint value creation while providing high-quality technology solution services.

　 2) Joint creation with business partners
　

We will strengthen the partnership with our business partners following the concept that partners are essential structural components in joint value creation.

　 3) Joint creation with our employees
　

Based on our belief that the development of human resources is part of our company's mission, we have a goal to invest in our employees while, at the same time, help the company grow.

  4) Joint creation with local communities
 

Focusing on a goal of enhancing our company's corporate reputation, we will contribute to the development of local communities.

Recruitment

JM Technology Indonesia (registered legally as PT JMT Indonesia), a Jakarta-based subsidiary of Japan's JM Technology, Inc, and a part of Mamezo Group which is a group company listed on Tokyo Stock Exchange, is an IT solutions provider focusing on leading edge mobile-centric IT solutions based on Japan's advanced technology and deep knowhow.
We at JM Technology Indonesia leverages high quality Indonesian IT talents and work directly with global engineering teams in Japan and therefore has direct access to Japanese technology and knowhow as well as the market.
We are making positions available for young, highly skilled and experienced Indonesian programmers to work at its soon-to-open Jakarta office. Successful candidates will work closely with multinational engineers in Japan online, as well as by going to Japan on project basis. Successful candidates will have the chance to be assigned to work at group company office located in prestigious area in Tenjin, Fukuoka as well as in Shinjuku, Tokyo.

GENERAL REQUIREMENTS
Candidate should be skilled and experienced in mobile and/or web development. Candidate should have strong basic knowledge of software engineering, and therefore is a quick learner of any new software technology. Candidates should be
Candidate should possess inquisitive mind and “can do” attitude, able to work with full commitment to deliver the job's target and deadline. Candidates should be self-motivated and able to work well in a team and with minimum supervision.
A working English knowledge is required. Japanese language knowledge is a big plus, and increases the chance to be assigned to work in Japan.


SPECIFIC REQUIREMENTS

ASP.NET DEVELOPER

This position will be responsible for planning, designing and developing web-based online systems and services.


Technical Qualification:
- 2 years or more real experience in developing web applications
- Skilled and experienced with ASP.NET (C#/VB.NET)
- Capable to create application and/or DLL components using C#/VB.NET
- Familiar with latest web technologies and frameworks (HTML/CSS/JavaScript/jQuery etc)
- Knowledgeable in writing and coding SQL statements
- Possess good debugging skills and problem solving skills
- Able to read and comprehend open source projects
- Skill of other programming languages is a plus (PHP, Java, C/C++, Ruby on Rails, Perl etc)
