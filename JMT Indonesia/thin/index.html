Work from home securely with AirScope2

  • turns a PC anywhere into thin client using just one USB memory stick/li>
  • safely access office's PC environment from home or on a business trip
  • prevent information leakage from office's devices brought out from the office
  • installation is as simple as putting controller box at the office
  • extremely low cost
  • supports Wake On LAN (remote power turn-on)

Usage scenario

This product allows safe access to internal system and data while at client's premises or on business trip. Those in business trips, giving presentation out of the office, working on client's premises etc will find this product very helpful. Regular access to internal applications and data will become possible as well.

System overview

Just insert the "USB client" into the PC and turn it on. It will start a VPN connection and exchange SSH encryption key for safe access to office PC. No data is stored on the PC, so even if the PC is stolen there will be no information leakage.

System configuration

This thin system can be installed just by putting a controller box at existing LAN.
